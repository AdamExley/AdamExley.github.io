About Me

I invite you to check out my other pages (above) for more directed information, but here's a general bio:

I'm Adam Exley, a Sophomore at The Ohio State University studying Electrical and Computer Engineering in the Honors Program.

I like to learn as much as I can about whatever I can get my hands on. In school this has helped me excel in physics, calculus, and other STEM fields, but it's a little more noticeable outside of the classroom. Once something gets a hold of my interest, I'm going to pursue it. So far this has led me to get into fixing electronics (like DSLR cameras), arranging music, photography, 3D printing, and even programming in Python - just to name a few things. Who knows what it'll be next? (I sure don't)

Overall, most of my interests have usually fell along the lines of electronics and technology, which is why I decided to study Electrical and Computer Engineering. Aside from my coursework, I've also had the opportunity to engage in research at Ohio State through the Center for Design and Manufacturing Excellence. I've primarily focused on how Artificial Intelligence can be implemented in different settings. More details are available under the Projects page.

Music

Music has always been a large part of my life ever since I began playing trumpet in 4th grade.
Since then I've learned how to play other brass instruments like Flugelhorn and Euphonium, and have attempted to learn a few others (with varying degrees of motivation and success).

Arrangement and Recordings

Besides merely playing music, I have also enjoyed arranging music (rewriting it for my own instrumentation).
On top of this, I know how to record, edit, and mix music in a Digital Audio Workspace (DAW).

The Experimental Things

Even in my musical endeavors I have always been an engineer at heart (to some degree).
Because of that, it may not be too surprising to know that I've 3D printed both a violin and viola, have made my own ribbon microphone, and have even restored a couple trumpets.
